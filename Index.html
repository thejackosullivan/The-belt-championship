The legend
"Shall we play for this?"
It was an ordinary round at Boughton Golf Club, Kent. Sam Johnson reached into his bag and produced a camo canvas belt — cannabis buckle and all — belonging to one Frank Rennie. Nobody said no. And so the most prestigious prize in Kent golf was born.

Each champion earns the right to sew their initials and year of victory onto the belt as a patch. The belt carries its own history. This website carries the rest.
